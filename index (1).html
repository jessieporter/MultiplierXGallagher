📋UAE Statutory Requirements — Know Before You Hire
13th & 14th Month Pay
Not legally mandated but market standard and widely expected. Failure to include in offer packages significantly hurts recruitment and retention in the UAE talent market.
End of Service Gratuity (EOSG)
Mandatory after 1 year. 21 days' basic salary/year for first 5 years, then 30 days/year. Must be paid on exit. Multiplier accrues monthly — no surprise lump sums.
Notice Period
30–90 days per contract (Federal Decree-Law No. 33 of 2021). Probationary employees: 7–14 days. All Multiplier contracts are compliant.
Annual Leave
30 calendar days per year after 1 year. Pro-rated in year one. Cannot be forfeited — must be taken or compensated.
Mandatory Health Insurance
Dubai (DHA regulation) requires employer-provided health insurance for all employees. Multiplier partners with Dubai Insurance to provide compliant coverage.
Visa & Work Permit Sponsorship
All expatriate employees need a UAE residence visa + work permit. Multiplier manages full visa sponsorship through the owned entity. Allow 2–3 weeks lead time alongside onboarding.

📋Mexican Statutory Requirements — Know Before You Hire
Aguinaldo (13th Month — Mandatory)
Legally required under Federal Labor Law. Minimum 15 days' salary paid before December 20. Many employers pay 30+ days competitively. Multiplier invoices monthly to smooth cash flow.
PTU — Profit Sharing
10% of annual taxable profit distributed to employees by May 31 each year. First-year employees excluded. Multiplier calculates and distributes PTU on behalf of Gallagher.
IMSS / INFONAVIT (~25–30%)
Employer contributes to health/disability (IMSS), housing (INFONAVIT), and retirement (SAR). Approximately 25–30% on top of salary — all remitted by Multiplier.
Vacation + Prima Vacacional (25%)
Minimum 12 days in Year 1, scaling by 2 days/year. A mandatory 25% vacation premium (Prima Vacacional) is also owed on top — a widely missed obligation.
Termination (Without Cause)
90-day indemnity + 20 days/year worked + seniority premium. Multiplier structures all exits to comply with Federal Labor Law.
Wet Ink Signatures (Critical)
Mexican labor law requires physical wet ink signatures on contracts — electronic signatures are invalid for labor purposes. Budget minimum 5 business days. Multiplier manages the physical signing process.

📋Kenyan Statutory Requirements — Know Before You Hire
NSSF (Pension)
Employer contributes KES 200/month (legacy) or up to 6% of pensionable earnings under the 2013 Act (subject to ongoing court proceedings). Multiplier stays current with all rulings.
SHIF (Health Insurance — 2024)
Transitioning from NHIF to the Social Health Insurance Fund (SHIF). Both employer and employee contribute 2.75% of gross salary. Multiplier is already fully compliant with the 2024 rollout.
Housing Levy (2023)
Under the Affordable Housing Act: 1.5% employer + 1.5% employee of gross monthly salary. Introduced 2023 — many EOR platforms are still catching up. Multiplier includes this in all Kenyan payroll runs.
Annual Leave
Minimum 21 working days per year. Leave not taken within 12 months must be compensated. Multiplier tracks accrual and proactively flags upcoming liability.
Notice Period
Up to 28 days under the Employment Act 2007 — or longer if specified in the contract. Multiplier ensures all contracts meet or exceed the statutory minimum.
Service Pay / Gratuity
For employees not in a registered pension scheme: 15 days' wages per year of service owed on termination. Multiplier tracks and discloses this obligation on every monthly invoice.

📋Peruvian Statutory Requirements — Know Before You Hire
Gratificación — 13th Month (July)
Legally required. One full month's gross salary by July 15. Includes an additional 9% EsSalud bonus on the gratificación. Multiplier invoices monthly to smooth cost.
Gratificación — 14th Month (December)
Legally required. One full month's gross salary by December 15. Same 9% EsSalud bonus applies. Both are pro-rated for mid-semester hires — Multiplier calculates automatically.
CTS (Severance Fund)
1/12 of salary + 1/6 of bonuses deposited into the employee's personal bank account every May and November. Acts as pre-funded severance — fully managed by Multiplier.
EsSalud (9% Health)
Employer pays 9% of monthly gross salary to the national health system. Employees get full medical coverage from Day 1. Remitted by Multiplier every payroll cycle.
Vacation (30 days — Best in LATAM)
30 calendar days per year — the most generous leave entitlement in Latin America. Must be taken within 1 year or compensated. Multiplier tracks accrual and flags obligations proactively.
Termination Indemnity
Unjustified dismissal triggers 1.5 months per year worked (capped at 12 months) under Legislative Decree 728. Multiplier provides full exit structuring and compliance guidance.
